🔐 NETCRYPT is a secure offline-capable encryption and messaging app.

KEY SECURITY FEATURES:
✅ RSA-2048/4096 asymmetric encryption
✅ AES-256-GCM with authentication
✅ PBKDF2 password derivation
✅ SecureRandom entropy
✅ HmacSHA256 and SHA-256
✅ Digital signatures (message authenticity)
✅ Perfect Forward Secrecy via ECDH
✅ Expiring messages and Burn After Reading
✅ Sensitive memory wipe (3x overwrite)
✅ Secure clipboard, app lifecycle wiping

Only you can access your data — no accounts, no tracking, no cloud. Ideal for those who take privacy seriously.
